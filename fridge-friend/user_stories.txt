As a consumer on a diet, I want an app that will help me plan my meals in advance so that I can create a shopping list that will help me plan accordingly.

As a habitual saver, I want an app that will help me keep track of what is in my fridge so that I can avoid purchasing the same item twice if I am at the store and forget if I have an ingredient.

As a consumer who hates waste, I want to an app that will remind me what food is in my fridge so that I can plan meals that will incorporate that item before it goes bad.
